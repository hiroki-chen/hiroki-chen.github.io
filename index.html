About

Haobin Chen (Hiroki) is a fourth-year undergraduate student at Nankai University, majoring in information security in the College of Computer Science and Cybersecurity. Currently, he is doing remote internship on TEE and verification and being advised by Dr. Mingshen Sun and Prof. Xiaofeng Wang. He was previously advised by Prof. Zheli Liu.

His research interests lie in the area of computer security, and system security, especially the intersecting point of theoretical and practical security.
